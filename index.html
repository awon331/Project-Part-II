Purpose

Think Global strives to enforce respect, acceptance and appreciation of the cultural diversity that exists in the world. Think Global envisions a world where everyone is free to have their own convictions and accepts that others have the right to live in peace, regardless of the diversity of their beliefs, identity or way of life.
